Our Services

"We believe a garden is a living architectural extension of the home—a sanctuary where precision meets the wild."

Arboretum Studio approaches every project as a unique ecosystem. Our process integrates structural integrity with botanical artistry, ensuring each landscape evolves beautifully through the seasons while respecting local ecology and your personal lifestyle.

01 — Structural Intent

Garden Design

Comprehensive master-planning that defines the bones of your outdoor space. From initial conceptualization to detailed construction drawings, we manage the architectural flow of terraces, paths, and water features.

  • Site Analysis & Topography
  • 3D Visualization & Walkthroughs
  • Technical Engineering Drawings
02 — Living Palette

Planting Schemes

Curated botanical compositions that prioritize texture, seasonal interest, and ecological sustainability. We select species that thrive in your micro-climate while creating a visual narrative.

"A planting plan is not just about color; it is about the sound of wind in the grasses and the fragrance of dusk."

03 — Curation

Landscape Styling

The final layer of luxury. We source bespoke outdoor furniture, specify sophisticated lighting systems, and curate artisanal containers to complete the garden's atmospheric experience.
